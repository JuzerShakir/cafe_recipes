Tasty Juicy Strawberries!!

Ingredients

  • 10 Strawberries.
  • 4 tablespoon - White or Black Chocolate.
  • 5 tablespoon - Colored Sugar (blue is best when using white chocolate).
  • 2 teaspoon - Vegetable Oil.

Directions

  1. Wash the Strawberries.
  2. Add the Vegetable Oil to the Chocolate and heat in the microwave from 20 to 30 seconds.
  1. Using a fork, mix the Chocolate well until you have a smooth melted Chocolate.
  2. Deep one Strawberry at a time in the Chocolate.
  1. Wait some second for the Chocolate to harden a little and the dip it in the colored Sugar.
  1. Repeat for all Strawberries.

And that's it, you're done!!
